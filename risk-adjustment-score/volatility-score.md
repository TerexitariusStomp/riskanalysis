# Volatility Score

**Volatilty Score is Composed Of The Price Change Over Different Time Periods**

**Volatility Score= (A+B+C+D+E/5)\*.01**

**Where**&#x20;

**A is 24 Hours**

**B is 1 week**

**C is 30 Days**

**D is 60 Days**&#x20;

**E is 90 Days**

**.01 is Applied to Bring Percent Score into Decimal**
